    Common Questions
    Frequently Asked Questions
    What is the minimum project value HDG accepts?
    HDG primarily engages with projects above ₹1 Crore. As a Class 1A CPWD contractor we are structured for mid-to-large scale government and institutional work. Smaller packages are evaluated case-by-case for strategic clients.
    Which states does HDG operate in?
    HDG has active delivery experience across Delhi, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Jammu & Kashmir, and Ladakh. Pan-India requirements are evaluated per-project.
    Does HDG handle both civil and MEP under one contract?
    Yes. Single-contract delivery covering Civil, MEP, Interiors, and Specialized Systems is one of HDG's core differentiators — eliminating the coordination gap that causes most delays.
    Is HDG eligible for CPWD, NBCC, and central agency tenders?
    Yes. HDG holds Class 1A CPWD registration — the highest classification — making the firm eligible for all central government contracts without a value ceiling. We have active empanelment with NBCC, DSIIDC, HSIIDC, and others.
    What documents are available for pre-qualification?
    HDG can provide: CPWD Class 1A certificate, company profile, project completion certificates, ESI/PF compliance, GST registration, PAN, audited financials, and a technical capability statement.
    What is HDG's current project availability?
    HDG is accepting new tenders for FY 2025–26. We maintain capacity for 3–5 concurrent large projects with dedicated project manager assignments. Contact us for an honest assessment.
